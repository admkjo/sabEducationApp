Educational Institution:

This is the page where the basic information regarding the school is entered. Information like school number and name, date of registration school contact number, and the likes can be saved and also changed on this page. This is where the school’s master username and password can also be entered and also changed.

      • To save the information, you have to fill it in the spaces provide for them in accordance and afterwards you click save.
      • To edit the basic information already entered, you have to go to the search column and click the search button. It will provide the school information and you click on select. You can then edit the fields which you want to change and click on ‘edit’ afterwards to save it.
