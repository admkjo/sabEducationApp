School House:

This page helps you create the various houses in the school. You can also edit or delete the house details already given.

To add a house, type the name and choose the gender of the inmates and the house master/mistress. Clicking ‘save’ adds the house to the database.

To edit the details of a house, search for the house using any of the search parameters and click on ‘select’. You can then change the details given and click on ‘edit’ to save. Clicking on ‘delete’ removes the data of the house selected.
