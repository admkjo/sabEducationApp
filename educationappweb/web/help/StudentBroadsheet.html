Student Broadsheet:

This page is used mainly for the generation of the students’ exam broadsheet. You can generate a broadsheet for the mid-term examination, mock examination or the regular general examination.

      • You have to choose the year group, programe, school class, examination type, and then click on ‘Generate broadsheet’ to generate.
      • Clicking on ‘Report Broadsheet’ will view the broadsheet in pdf format and give you the option of printing it out.
      • Clicking on ‘Student Without Mark’ button will print out the list of students by courses without their marks.
