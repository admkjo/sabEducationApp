Head Report Comment:

This page is for the head of the school to also make his/her remarks on each student’s report.

  1. Choose the year group, programme, class and student on whose report you want to work and it will display the academic report of the student in particular.
  2. You can then choose from the remark option a predefined remark and click ‘add’ to comment.
  3. When done, you can click ‘next’ to go to the next student in the class.
