Student Bill:

This is where the student’s bills are prepared. You can prepare a bill for all year groups collectively or individually or even for all classes collectively or individually depending on your own choosing. You can also edit or delete individual bill items already saved.

      • To prepare the student’s bills;
  1. Choose the year group, programme and class to be billed.
  2. You then choose the bill type and bill item.
  3. State the amount to be paid by all day students and that of all boarding house students.
  4. You then choose by gender who the bill item should affect.
  5. Click on ‘save’.

*Ticking the ’Apply to all year group’ box overrides the year group you chose and applies the bill to all year groups.

*Ticking the ‘Apply to all Classes’ box overrides the class you chose and applies the bill to all classes under the programme you chose.

      • You can view the bill of a class by clicking on ‘Load Bill for Selected Class’ after choosing the class.
      • When the bill is loaded, you can then edit on individual bill items by clicking on ‘select’ from its far right column and editing the details when loaded into the editing panel. Clicking on ‘edit’ saves the edited copy of the bill item whiles ‘delete’ removes the bill item from the selected class’ bill.
      • Clicking on ‘report Bill for selected Class’ prepares the report and lets you print out the bill for all students in that class.
      • Clicking the ‘Apply Bill to student ledger account’ effects all changes in the students’ ledger account.
