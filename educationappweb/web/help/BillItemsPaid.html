Bill Items Paid:

This page gives you the details of the bill items that have been paid by all the students or students from a particular class on a daily or monthly basis.

      • To view the report;
  1. Choose the bill item, the date, report type and the class.
  2. Click on ‘Load Summary’.
  3. Clicking on ‘Report’ lets you print the report out.
