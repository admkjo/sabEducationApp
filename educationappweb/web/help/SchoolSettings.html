School Settings:

On this page, you can change the settings of certain aspects of the school software. The student bill footnote option allows you to write a footnote on all the bills of the students in the school. You can also choose if regular exam marks and regular mock exam marks can be entered. Typing yes allows the marks to be entered whilst typing no chooses otherwise. You should therefore note that, upon any changes to the settings, you have to click on the ‘Update School Settings’ button to effect the changes made.
