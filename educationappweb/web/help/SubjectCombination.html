Subject Combination:

This is where you choose the various subject combinations in the school. You can also edit the various subject combinations already saved and also delete them if necessary.

To create a subject combination,

  1. Choose the combination programme.
  2. Type in the name of the combination and its short name.
  3. You then choose its status.
  4. Click on ‘save’.

After saving the combination name, you can choose it from the ‘Combination Subject for’ panel and choose the various subjects to be studied under this combination by checking the boxes before them. Click on ‘Update Combination Subject’ when done to save.

To edit the details of a combination,

  1. Search for the combination name by using any of the search parameters.
  2. Click on ‘select’ from the far right column before it and it should appear in the editing panel.
  3. You can then change the details of the combination.
  4. Click ‘edit’ when done to save the edited copy.
  5. Clicking ‘delete’ removes the subject combination data from the software.
