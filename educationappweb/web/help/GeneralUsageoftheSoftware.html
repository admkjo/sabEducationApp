GENERAL USAGE OF THE SOTWARE

Once you log in as the administrator, it gives you the privileges to access the software and update the content.

Some of the tools you see after logging in include;

  • General Actions
  • Examination Related
  • Year/ Term Activities
  • Set Up
  • Student Finances
  • Audit Tray
  • SMS Account

Each of these tools have pages that can help you update the contents and depending on what you want to update, you click on any of them and you will have access to the right page.
