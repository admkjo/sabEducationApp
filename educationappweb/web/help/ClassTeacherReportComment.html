Class Teacher Report Comment:

On this page, the Class teacher or Form master can comment on the students’ reports. It gives you the option of entering the attendance record of the student and the conduct, interest, attitude and remarks of the students.

To comment on the reports;

  1. Select your class, student list and select a student’s report on which to work.
  2. Choose the student conduct, Interest, Attitude, Remarks from the predefined terms.
  3. Click on ‘save’ when done.
  4. After commenting on the report, you can click on ‘Previous Student’ to go back to the previous student report or click ‘Next Student’ to go to the next report.
