Academic Term:

On this page, you can set the current or default academic term. It also gives you the option of adding, deleting or editing a specific academic term.

To set the current academic term, search for the term by using any of the search parameters and click on ‘select’ on the far right column of the chosen term. You can then click ‘Set selected Term as Current Term’ to choose the current term. You can click on ‘delete’ to delete the selected term and you can also edit the details of the selected term and click on ‘Edit’ afterwards to save.

To add a new academic term, choose the academic year from and term. Then select the start and end dates of the term and also the dates for the exams to start and end in that academic term you are creating. Click on ‘save’ when done to add the new academic term.
