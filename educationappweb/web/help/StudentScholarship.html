Student Scholarship:

On this page, you can view the list of scholarship students either in the whole school or in a class. This is the page where you can also assign students who have been awarded scholarships to their various scholarship categories.

      • To view the scholarship list of the whole school, choose the scholarship category and click ‘View Members’ or ‘Print Report’ to print the list out. To view the scholarship list of a class, you have to choose both the scholarship category and the class.
      • To assign a student to a scholarship category, enter the student ID and choose the scholarship category. After that, choose the date signed and click ‘Execute’.
