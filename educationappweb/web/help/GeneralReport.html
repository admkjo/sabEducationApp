General Report:

This is the page where general reports can be checked. You can check a student’s transcript, view the student age distribution, student class and subject distribution, and crosscheck the exam marks filling list.

Whist clicking the ‘Student class distribution’ button provides you with the male/female proportions of each class; the ‘Student Subject distribution’ button gives you the proportion of students studying each subject being taught in the school.

The ‘exam marks filling crosscheck list’ helps you to crosscheck the number of students in each subject in every class whose exam and class marks have already been entered.

To retrieve a student’s transcript, you have to enter the student’s ID in the space provided and click on the ‘Retrieve Transcript’ button.
