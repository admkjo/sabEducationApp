Student Mid Term Exam Marks:

This page allows you to enter the marks of the students from their Mid Term Exams and also to effect corrections.

  1. Choose the subject and the class and click on ‘Load Student Marks’. This gives you the class list with a space provided to type in the marks.
  2. Click ‘Save All’ when done to effect the changes made.
