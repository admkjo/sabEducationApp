Discipline Record:

On this page, you can keep track of all the disciplinary records in the school. You can enter details of a disciplined student and save them and can also delete a record when necessary.

To record, you choose the year group, programme and class of the student and key in his/her Id number. You then choose the disciplinary record item and key in the details in the space provided. You then comment on the disciplinary action and select the date of occurrence. Click on ‘save’ to save the information.

To delete a disciplinary record, search for the entry by using any of the search parameters. Click on ‘select’ from the far right of the entry to be deleted. Click on ‘delete’ and then ‘ok’ to confirm the deletion.
