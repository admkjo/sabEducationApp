Student Contacts:

This page allows you to generate the contact list of all students in a particular class in the school and also gives you the option of printing them out.

To generate the contact list, you have to choose the year group, school programme and the class. After choosing these options, you then click on the ‘General Student Contact’ button. This provides the name, programme, contact, gender, guardian name, guardian address and relation of all the students in the class chosen.

You can click on the ‘Print Contact’ button to print the contact list.
