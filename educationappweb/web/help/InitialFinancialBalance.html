Initial Financial Balance:

This page lets you see all the financial transactions of a student in the school. You can also debit or credit a student account from this page and delete a transaction when necessary.

To view all transactions by a student, enter the student’s id and click on ‘Load Student Ledger’.

To add a transaction to the ledger, choose the type of entry, either Debit or Credit balance, type in the amount involved and chooses for which bill it’s supposed to settle. Click on ‘save’ to add the transaction.

To edit an entry, click ‘select’ from the far right in line with the transaction and edit the details as necessary. Clicking ‘edit’ saves the edited information whilst clicking ‘delete’ removes the transaction.
