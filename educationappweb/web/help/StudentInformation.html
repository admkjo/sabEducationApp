Student Information:

This page gives you the opportunity to add the personal information, admission information, guardian information and even the picture of a student. It also allows you to edit the information of an already existing student.

To add the information of a new student, fill in the necessary details on this page, add the picture of the student and click ‘save’.

To edit the information of an already existing student, you have to search for the student’s information on the system before editing it. You can either search by the student’s ID, surname, other names, gender, boarding status, guardian name, programme offered, current class, subject combination or status of the student. Since the student ID is unique, it is advisable to use that option if available. After the option is chosen, enter the search term in the search text space provided n click on ‘search’. This provides all the information about all the students with the same search term you used so you can choose the one you want to update. After updating on the information of the student, click on ‘edit’ and the information will be updated.

Clicking on ‘update boarding status from last term’ button will update the boarding status and make all students who were boarders from the previous term and have not yet been changed to boarders for the current term.
