Student Ledger:

This is the page used to enter any financial obligations being made by a student. You can also edit the details of a transaction or delete the transaction entirely.

      • To record a transaction;
  1. Enter the student Id.
  2. Click on ‘Load Student Ledger’. This will produce a record of the student’s transactions and show any outstanding balance.
  3. You can then select the date of transaction and enter the amount involved.
  4. You type in the person settling the bill.
  5. Choose the medium of payment and then type in the medium of payment number.
  6. You can then click on ‘save’ to store the transaction.
  7. After saving the transaction, search for it in the ledger and click on ‘select’ from its far right column. Clicking on ‘Generate Receipt’ will print out a receipt of the transaction.
      • You can also edit the transaction by changing the values and clicking on ‘edit’ to save the new values.
      • Clicking on delete removes the transaction from the ledger.
      • Clicking on ‘View Student Ledger Detail’ prints out all transactions of the student in the ledger.
