Staff Teaching Classes:

This is where teachers are assigned to subjects and classes for the academic year.

  1. Go to the search column and search for the staff by using any of the search options, i.e. staff id, surname, other names, gender, etc.
  2. Select the staff you want to assign by clicking on ‘Select’ from the far right of the person’s column.
  3. You can then choose the subject and the class you want to assign the teacher for the academic year or term.
  4. When done, click on the ‘save’ button.
