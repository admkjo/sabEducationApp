School Staff:

You can add the information of all the staffs in the school including professional and non professional teaching staffs and non teaching staffs. It also gives you the option to edit the information of some staffs. You can also print out the staff information list.

      • To add a staff’s information, fill in the details of the staff upon getting to this screen and when done, click on save.
      • To edit,
  1. Search for the staff by choosing the search term you want to use and type the detail in the search text column.
  2. After that, click on search.
  3. Look through the search results and click on ‘select’ on the far right of the staff you want to edit. The details of the staff are then displayed and can therefore be edited.
  4. After editing, click on ‘edit’ to effect the changes made.
      • You can click on ‘In Service Staff Summary Info’ at anytime to print out the details of all the staffs entered
