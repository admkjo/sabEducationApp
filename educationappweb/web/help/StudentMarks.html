Student Marks:

This page allows you to view the marks of the students in a particular class. You can also enter their marks from here and also change it if it’s already entered.

      • To view the marks;
  1. Choose the subject and the class.
  2. Click on ‘Load Student Marks’ to load. This generates the class list and gives you the option of entering the class marks and exam marks.
