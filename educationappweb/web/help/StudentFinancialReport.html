Student Financial Report:

This page gives you the financial report of the students either in a class or in the school as a whole. You can view either the debit balance or the credit balance.

The access panel on the left gives you a report of the class whiles that on the right gives you a report of the whole school.

      • To get the class financial report;
  1. Choose the year group, programme and class.
  2. Choose whether to produce a debit or a credit balance report.
  3. Click on ‘Prepare Summary’ to produce the report.
      • To get the school financial report;
  1. Choose whether to produce a debit or a credit balance report.
  2. Click on ‘Prepare All Summary’.
      • Clicking on ‘Report’ on either case will produce a printout of the financial report.
