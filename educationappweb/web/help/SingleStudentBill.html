Single Student Bill:

This page allows you to prepare a bill for a single student.

      • To prepare the student’s bill;
  1. Search for the student by choosing any of the search parameters.
  2. Click on ‘select’ from its far right column. This loads the student’s details in the Selected Student panel.
  3. You can then start preparing the bill by choosing the bill type, bill item and typing the amount.
  4. Click on ‘save’ and continue with the next bill item until done.
