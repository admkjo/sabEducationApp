Promotion:

This page helps you to change the promotion status of students. You can promote students from one level to the other. This feature also helps you choose if a student is on probation, repeated or withdrawn from the school.

      • To choose their promotion status;
  1. Choose the education level, school programme and class.
  2. Click on ‘Load class members’ to load the class list.
  3. After the class list has been loaded, you can then change the promotion status of the individual students in the class and if promoted, to which class.
  4. Click on ‘Save all promotion status’ to save and ‘Effect All Promotions’ to move them to their various classes.
